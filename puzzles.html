Horse Racing

Imagine you want to buy 3 race horses. (You want winners so that means you want the fastest 3 horses possible)

The owner of the horses is a sly man and won't allow you to time the horses.

Instead he allows you as many races as you want to find the horses you wish to buy (at a price per race of course).

He has 25 horses and you can race 5 horses at a time.

What is the minimum number of races for you to know which horses are the fastest? (and therefore the cheapest way)


Assume that the horses will ALWAYS run at the same speed.
