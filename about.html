My Statistical Life (now in HD)

About Me

My name is Laura Gemmell. I am a recent graduate and a lover of maths and statistics.

I graduated with a First in Mathematics, Statistics and Operational Reseach from Queens University, Belfast in July 2014.

After graduation I was all set to become an accountant and then the horror of giving up something I was so passionate about (maths and stats) hit me.

A few job changes later (forensic technology and market research mainly), I am working as an Insight Analyst and all set to start a Masters in Applied Statistics at Birkbeck in September!

About This Blog

I decided that I need an outlet for my interest and in this day and age everything is online, so a website seemed like an obvious choice.

I'm going to use this opportunity to improve my writing, teaching, coding and research skills.

I am hoping that it'll be useful to someone some day, whether it's a student who's stuck on a topic or just someone with some time to kill.
